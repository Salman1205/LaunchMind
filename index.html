Task Posting

Startup founders can post small coding tasks with a clear description, requirements, and a fixed payment amount.

Task Claiming

Freelance developers can claim tasks that match their skills and interests, and commit to completing them within 48 hours.

Time-Limited Task Completion

Developers have 48 hours to complete tasks after claiming them. The platform will enforce this deadline and penalize late completions.

Rating and Feedback System

Founders and developers can rate and provide feedback on each other after task completion. Ratings will be based on code quality, communication, and timeliness.

Payment Processing

The platform will handle payment processing, escrow, and disbursement to developers upon task completion and approval.

Task Categorization and Filtering

Tasks will be categorized by programming language, complexity, and other relevant criteria, allowing developers to filter and find tasks that match their skills and interests.
