GigHub

Connect with freelance developers to complete small coding tasks within 48 hours

Features

Task Posting

Founders can post small coding tasks (max 10 lines of code, estimated completion time < 2 hours) with a budget and deadline (48 hours).

Task Claiming

Developers can claim tasks that match their skills (self-assessed through a skills test) and availability. A developer can claim a maximum of 3 tasks at a time.

Task Completion & Review

Developers complete tasks within the given deadline. Founders review and rate the work (1-5 stars) based on quality and timeliness.

Developer Profile & Skills Test

Developers create profiles showcasing their skills, experience, and ratings. A skills test (multiple-choice questions) assesses their proficiency in specific programming languages.

Founder Dashboard & Task Management

Founders can post multiple tasks (max 5 at a time), track progress, and view developer ratings and reviews. They can also cancel or edit tasks before the deadline.

Payment Processing & Escrow

GigHub holds payment in escrow until the task is completed and rated. Upon successful completion, payment is released to the developer.
