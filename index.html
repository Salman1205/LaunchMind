GigHub

Accelerate your product development by outsourcing small coding tasks to freelance developers.

Features

  • Task Posting: Allow startup founders to create and post small coding tasks with specific requirements and deadlines.
  • Task Claiming: Enable freelance developers to claim and accept tasks that match their skills and interests.
  • Task Completion Tracking: Provide a system for tracking task progress and completion, including automated reminders and notifications.
  • Payment Processing: Integrate a secure payment gateway to facilitate transactions between startup founders and freelance developers.
  • Rating and Review System: Implement a rating and review system to ensure high-quality work and accountability among users.
  • Skill and Interest Matching: Develop an algorithm to match freelance developers with tasks that align with their skills and interests.
  • Task Dispute Resolution: Establish a process for resolving disputes between startup founders and freelance developers, including mediation and escrow services.
  • Analytics and Insights: Provide data and insights on task completion rates, payment amounts, and user engagement to inform product development and marketing strategies.
